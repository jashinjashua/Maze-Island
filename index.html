Story Overview

Jaja-kun and his love Mryl-chan was living together in the peaceful island until Boss Emerald Golem kidnapped her.The name of island which the girl kidnapped by golems is Maze Island.

There are a lots of dangerous golems who attack our brave hero but he is determined to save the girl with the magic potions he found on the island.

Let's help Jaja-kun and while trying to save the girl don't forget to collect the loots ^^

What's on the island ?

Types of Enemies

There are two types of enemies in the maze and one boss in the final room

  • Stone Golem - Has 2 hp and throws a stone that reduces 1 hp from the player.
  • Gold Golem - Has 5 hp and throws a Gold stone that reduces 2 hp from the player.
  • Boss Golem- Has "Unknown" hp and throws a Emerald stone that reduces 3 hp from the player.

Types of Upgrades

There are 4 types of Upgrade Potions you can take in the maze

  • Hp Potion - Increases Hp level
  • Max-Hp Potion - Increses the player's HP to the Highest level
  • Speed Potion - Increases the movement speed of players by three
  • Gun Potion - There is only "One Bottle" in the entire map. if you take it you can shot the Golems.You have to find this for fighting the Boss Golem.

Types of Loots

There are 3 types of Loots you can found in the maze

  • Ring - Often to find - Value = 50 points
  • Ruby - Rare to find - Value = 250 points
  • Diamond - Very rare to find - Value = 400 points

Game Controls

  •        Classical game control : W : up - A : left - D : right - S : down
  •   Left Click : Shoots 1 direction where the mouse is. Reload: 0.50 sec.
  •   Right Click (Special bullets) : Shoots 12 direction (360 degree). Reload: 2.5 sec.
